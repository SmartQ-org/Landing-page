Contactless Queuing

Businesses across Canada are struggling to maintain customer satisfaction due to the new found emphasis placed on managing physical lines. SmartQ aims to solve this issue, utilizing innovative technology to transfer physical lines into an easy-to-manage electronic queuing system. Using SmartQ, customers are able to enter a virtual priority queue which also provides live positional updates. Allowing your customers liberation from the burden of physical lines and instead simply show up, walk-in, pay and leave.
